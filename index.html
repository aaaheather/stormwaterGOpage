Stormwater Training for Everyone

Stormwater is runoff from rain or snowstorms, that erodes landscapes and causes pollution

What is StormwaterGO?

stormwaterGO is an online learning management platform specific to the stormwater industry. stormwaterGO consists of several online training modules accessed at any time by users where attendance and progress is tracked and managed. Stormwater regulations require all stakeholders receive ongoing training. While there are high level trainings including national certification trainings online, only stormwaterGO delivers general training to non stormwater professionals that must receive training in order to meet training requirements.





What are stormwater fees, and how can I avoid them?

A stormwater fee is a cost imposed on real estate owners for pollution in stormwater drainage from impervious surface runoff. Taxes are proportional to the total impervious area on a particular property, including concrete or asphalt driveways and roofs, that do not allow rain to infiltrate. These charges can be costly which is why StormwaterGO offers online training courses at a discount to protect your organization from unwanted stormwater fees.
